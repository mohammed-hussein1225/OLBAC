Details

Ideal for studio, film, TV, and especially location shooting, the 3.57 x 2.6 x 2.6" MOLUS G60 Pocket COB Monolight from Zhiyun has COB LEDs and boasts a host of professional features in a very small package. The G60 has a wide variable color range of 2700 to 6500K with high CRI/TLCI 96/97 ratings and dims from 0 to 100%. Color and brightness can be adjusted on the fixture, but you can also make changes wirelessly using Zhiyun's app.

The G60 includes a multi-voltage AC adapter, power adapter organizer bag, and reflector. With the purchase of an optional USB-C charging cable, you can use PD technology to quickly power an optional attached battery for wireless use.

Zhiyun MOLUS G60 Mini/Pocket COB Light

The MOLUS G60 has 60W of power output supported by innovative cooling technology while serving as a universal film light.

Pro Lighting within Your Hand

Weighing only 10.6 oz and as small as a Rubik's Cube, the G60 brings you a brand-new lighting experience without cumbersome burdens.

DynaVort Cooling System

The G60 employs its DynaVort Cooling System consisting of gyroscope modeling heat sinks and FOC fans to prevent overheating. The DynaVort technology is based on the fluid dynamics and attitude-control algorithm, significantly raising the cooling efficiency through intelligent control over airflow emissions.

Adaptive Power Supply System

The adaptive integrated power supply system provides two charging solutions. You can either charge G60 via the supplied DC power adapter or an optional USB-C cable for fast charging which allows input power monitoring.

High CRI and TLCI

With a CRI of 96 and a TLCI of 97, the light gives you accurate color allowing you to easily adjust accurate color and brightness.

Live Stream

Zhiyun helps you build your own professional live stream studio with lighter, smaller, yet brighter equipment, thwarting location restrictions while bringing up-to-date lighting effects.

Bluetooth for Efficient Remote Control

No more fear of complicated setups and crew shortage. The G60 has onboard Bluetooth mesh control via its app, letting you explore wireless possibilities.
